RDAP Lookup

Notes:

  • To see which TLDs support RDAP, click here.
  • This page uses the RDAP.org service, so your queries are sent to that server, but the RDAP responses themselves come directly from the registry (or registrar) servers.
  • Queries for top-level domains and ICANN-accredited registrars do not use the IANA bootstrap registries; instead these queries are sent to the {root,registrars}.rdap.org servers.
  • To submit feedback, click here. Please contact the registries if you have any issues with the contents of RDAP responses.
  • The RDAP Web Client is Free Software; for the license, click here. To fork a copy of the git repository, click here.
